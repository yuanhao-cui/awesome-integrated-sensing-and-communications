# 📖 Surveys & Tutorials

> Comprehensive survey and tutorial papers on Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC).

The ISAC research landscape has been shaped by a growing body of survey papers spanning topics from fundamental signal processing and theoretical limits to application-specific domains such as vehicular networks, UAV systems, and IoT. This page curates the most influential surveys that have defined and advanced the ISAC field, with a focus on works cited in [Zhang et al., IEEE COMST, 2026](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.06830).

Masouros, D. Niyato, M.
