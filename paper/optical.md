# 🔦 Optical ISAC

> Optical ISAC extends sensing and communication beyond RF by exploiting the ultra-wide THz bandwidths and short wavelengths of the optical spectrum. This emerging paradigm encompasses Visible Light Communication (VLC), Free-Space Optics (FSO), and Photonic Sensing—each offering unique advantages in high-resolution sensing and high-speed data transmission. Hybrid RF-optical architectures further promise the best of both worlds for 6G networks.

## Visible Light Communication (VLC)

VLC leverages LEDs as transmitters and photodetectors as receivers to achieve simultaneous data transmission and environmental sensing. Recent works demonstrate indoor positioning, motion tracking, and imaging with centimeter-level accuracy.

## Free-Space Optics (FSO)

FSO employs infrared or laser beams for long-range communication and environmental sensing, supporting terabit-per-second data rates over free-space channels. Key challenges include atmospheric turbulence and weather sensitivity.
